Stackable Gemstone Rings

Build Your Own Ring Stack

These slim gemstone rings are designed to be mixed, matched, and stacked. Choose your favorite stones—such as amethyst, citrine, or rose quartz—and create a personalized look that tells your story.

Each ring is hand-formed, hammered for subtle texture, and polished to a soft shine. Wear just one for a minimalist look, or combine several for a bolder statement.

Details:

  • Available in sterling silver or gold-filled
  • Faceted 3–4 mm gemstone cabochons
  • Custom sizing available
  • Sold individually so you can build your own set
