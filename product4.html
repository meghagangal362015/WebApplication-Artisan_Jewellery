Pearl Horizon Bracelet

Classic Pearls with a Modern Twist

The Pearl Horizon Bracelet blends classic freshwater pearls with tiny metal accent beads for a look that feels both timeless and fresh. It sits comfortably on the wrist and layers beautifully with watches or bangles.

Each pearl is individually selected for luster and shape, then hand-strung on durable, flexible beading wire for long-lasting wear.

Details:

  • Freshwater pearls, 4–6 mm
  • Gold-filled or sterling silver accent beads
  • Spring ring or lobster clasp
  • Custom sizing available upon request
