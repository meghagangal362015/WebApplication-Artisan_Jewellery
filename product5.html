Boho Charm Anklet

Summer-Inspired, Beach-Ready

The Boho Charm Anklet is a playful mix of seed beads and tiny charms that instantly gives any outfit a relaxed, vacation-ready feel. Wear it barefoot on the sand or peeking out from under your favorite jeans.

Each anklet is strung by hand with a careful balance of color and texture, making every piece unique.

Details:

  • Glass seed beads and metal charms
  • Adjustable length with extender chain
  • Available in warm or cool color palettes
  • Finished with a secure lobster clasp
