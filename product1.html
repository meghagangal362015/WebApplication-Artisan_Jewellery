Rose Gold Filigree Necklace

Delicate Filigree, Everyday Elegance

This rose gold filigree necklace is handcrafted with intricate wirework inspired by vintage heirloom pieces. The lightweight pendant hangs from a fine, adjustable chain so you can wear it as a subtle accent or layered with other favorites.

Each necklace is made to order in small batches, ensuring attention to every curve and swirl in the design. It pairs beautifully with both casual outfits and special occasion dresses.

Details:

  • Rose gold–plated brass pendant and chain
  • Adjustable 16–18 inch chain length
  • Lobster clasp closure
  • Nickel-free and skin friendly
